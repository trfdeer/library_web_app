About

This is a library where you can borrow most IT and Computer Science Related Books. More free books will be added soon.

Usage

  1. Create a profile by going to the "Profile" page.
  2. You can borrow books by searching books in the "Browse" page.
  3. You can see your borrowed books in the "Profile" page.
  4. Books are issued for 30 days, and they can be further re-issued for 15 days.
  5. Only 5 books can be borrowed at a time.
